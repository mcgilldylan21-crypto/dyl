The problem

Why habits are so hard to keep

Building a new habit sounds easy — but life gets in the way. Most people fail not because they lack motivation, but because they lack a system.

😴

Forgetting to start

Without a timely reminder, even the most motivated person skips their habit. "I'll do it later" becomes never.

📉

No progress visibility

When you can't see how far you've come, it's easy to feel like nothing is working — and quit too early.

🔔

One-size reminders fail

Generic alarm apps don't adapt to your schedule. They go off at the wrong time and get ignored.

🤯

Too many apps, no focus

People switch between apps, notes, and reminders. There's no single smart place that brings it all together.
