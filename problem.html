The problem

Why habits are hard to keep

Starting a habit is easy. Keeping it going is harder because most people do not have reminders, feedback, or one place to track progress.

01

People forget

Busy schedules make it easy to skip habits even when the goal matters.

02

No clear progress

Without streaks or charts, users cannot easily see improvement over time.

03

Generic reminders fail

Standard alarms go off at the wrong time and quickly get ignored.

04

Too many tools

Notes, alarms, and calendars are split across apps instead of working together.

HabitIQ is the fix.

HabitIQ combines smart reminders, easy check-ins, and progress tracking so users can stay focused and build habits with less effort.
