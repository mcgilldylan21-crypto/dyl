Future roadmap

This page shows how HabitIQ could grow after the first version. It also gives your website a complete set of six pages.

Phase 1

Launch the core app

Dashboard, reminders, streak tracking, and weekly progress summaries.

Phase 2

Add personalization

Smarter AI suggestions based on routines, missed habits, and preferred times.

Phase 3

Build community features

Friend challenges, shared goals, and accountability groups for motivation.

Phase 4

Expand platforms

Smartwatch support, widgets, and deeper integrations with calendars and health apps.

Why this page helps

Adding a roadmap page makes the site feel more complete and shows teachers or viewers that the project has a clear vision beyond the first prototype.
